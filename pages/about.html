About us

IOT Networks provides custom web, cloud, mobile, digital, and desktop software development and consulting services to clients in every industry, from hot startups to big companies. Founded in 2016.

We carry out IT consultancy assignments and we develop your strategic applications… Differently

We Can Help You Design, Build and Operate Openshift, Kubernetes, And Docker. All in the cloud

Kubernetes is an open source orchestration system for Docker containers. It handles scheduling onto nodes in a compute cluster and actively manages workloads to ensure that their state matches the user’s declared intentions. Openshift is Redhat Openshift Container Platform is the industry’s most secure and comprehensive enterprise-grade container platform based on industry standards, Docker and Kubernetes Docker is designed to make it easier to create, deploy, and run applications by using containers

We are specialists in Containers, Kubernetes and Cloud

IotNetworks have a history of successful involvement in open source communities and projects, including Openshift,Kubernetes, and Docker. Whether it is general consultancy, training, hands on development, or help in transitioning your organisation to be able to use Kubernetes within a broader programme of work, IotNetworks can partner with you to make this a reality.

Typical Kubernetes Engagements

Consulting
